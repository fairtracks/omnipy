# 10 Superhero Names for the United Presentation Hero

Here are ten superhero name ideas that blend the visual, formatting, and UI capabilities of the ultimate presentation hero:

1. **Prisma** (Short, punchy, implies splitting light/data into multiple colors and panels)
2. **Spectra** (A nod to the 300+ color spectrum she wields)
3. **The Illuminatrix** (A slightly more comic-book/badass evolution of "The Illuminator")
4. **Chroma-Key** (Plays on color styling and unlocking/revealing hidden data)
5. **Panoptica** (The all-seeing; represents her ability to view multiple files side-by-side)
6. **Syntaxia** (Directly references her format-wide syntax highlighting powers)
7. **The Renderer** (A bit more industrial and heavy-hitting, like "The Punisher")
8. **Palette Prime** (Highlights her intense weaponization of personalized color styles)
9. **The Weaver** (Focuses on her ability to weave tables inside nested JSON panels)
10. **Visor** (A sleek, classic superhero name, focusing on how she lets the user *see* the invisible data)

Once you pick your favorite, we can unite the best backstory elements (the Fluffy Unicorn/Heavy Metal colors, nested panels, side-by-side diffs) into one final hero profile!
