# 10 Alternative Poster Abstract Concepts (Title + Ingress)

Here are 10 distinct, creative angles for the 2026 ELIXIR All-Hands poster. Since you're considering a comic-style layout, many of these are designed to have a punchy, narrative hook that would pair perfectly with visual storytelling.

---

### Idea 1: The Superhero / Comic Book Angle (Focus: Interactive Rollbacks & Safety)

**Title:**
Omnipy to the rescue: Saving bioinformatics pipelines from villainous data!  

**Ingress:**
*Boom! Pow!* Your machine learning pipeline just crashed because a REST API
sneaked a string into an integer field. Enter Omnipy: the strongly-typed,
auto-reverting superhero of data wrangling. Discover how interactive
rollbacks and strict "Parse, Don't Validate" principles can protect your
workflows from the absolute chaos of dynamic biological data.

**Comments:**
I like the superhero angle, but the focus on just the rollbacks is a bit
narrow.
