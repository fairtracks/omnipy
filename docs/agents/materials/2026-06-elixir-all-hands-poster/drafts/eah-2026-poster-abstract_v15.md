# ELIXIR All-Hands 2026 Abstract

## The Omnificents vs. The UnFAIR Alliance
### Crisis on Infinite Dataflows (and How Omnipy Solves It)

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming bioinformatics, but this new era introduces a massive vulnerability: AI is only as good as the data it consumes, and the data it produces cannot always be trusted. On the input side, AI models require clean, perfectly structured "diets," yet our biological databases are often filled with messy, tangled, and unstructured data. On the output side, generative AI agents frequently "hallucinate" fabricated properties or incorrect formats. If these bad inputs and fabricated outputs are not strictly controlled, they silently poison our downstream scientific dataflows.

"It's Static Typing!"… "It's Dynamic!"… "It's Omnipy!" Developed by ELIXIR Norway, Omnipy represents a paradigm shift: *dataflows, not workflows*. Instead of just orchestrating tasks and files, Omnipy is an interactive safety net designed to protect the data itself. It acts as a strict border control for your dataflow: it ensures that the metadata fed into your machine learning models is perfectly structured and validated, and it actively polices the outputs to instantly catch and block AI hallucinations before they can cause harm. It allows researchers to experiment freely, knowing their data integrity is constantly guarded.

To explain the technical architecture behind this data protection, our poster presents an epic, comic-book-style showdown: the "UnFAIR Alliance" (the villains of data decay) versus "The Omnificents" (the dataflow protectors). Attendees will see the technical battles play out as our heroes deploy Omnipy's core features:
* **The Endless Schema vs. "Honest" HAL Lucinator:** Discover how Omnipy uses strict type enforcement to act as an undeniable cosmic law, automatically banishing fabricated keys and AI hallucinations from metadata payloads.
* **The Merciless Mimic Mantis vs. The Null Ninja:** See how continuous validation and perfectly camouflaged Python structures protect interactive Jupyter sandboxes, instantly reverting data states the microsecond silent corruption is attempted.
* **Optimus Parse vs. Lord Franken-Format:** Watch chaotic, unstructured database payloads get elegantly parsed into AI-ready tabular formats through robust, declarative conversions.
* **Visor vs. Count Cryptic:** Marvel as Visor shatters unreadable, monochrome memory pointers by projecting data in its true multi-dimensional form, complete with auto-scaling nested panels and highly personalized syntax themes.

While The Omnificents are formidable, the war against the UnFAIR Alliance is far from over. Omnipy is an open, actively evolving framework, and we cannot protect the bioinformatics ecosystem alone. We invite developers, domain experts, and researchers across ELIXIR to join our heroic roster. Come collaborate with us to expand domain models, build new data plugins, and help forge the hallucination-proof foundation needed to safely scale the next generation of AI-assisted science.
