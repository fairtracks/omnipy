# ELIXIR All-Hands 2026 Abstract

## The Omnificents vs. The UnFAIR Alliance
### Crisis on Infinite Dataflows (and How Omnipy Saves the Day!)

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming bioinformatics, but introduces a massive vulnerability: AI is only as good as the data it consumes, and its generated outputs cannot always be trusted. While AI models require clean, perfectly structured data inputs, biological databases are often messy, tangled, and unstructured. Conversely, generative AI agents frequently hallucinate fabricated keys or incorrect formats. Without strict control, these bad inputs and fabricated outputs silently poison downstream scientific dataflows.

*"It's Static Typing!"… "It's Dynamic!"… "It's Omnipy!"*

Developed by ELIXIR Norway, Omnipy represents a paradigm shift: *dataflows, not workflows*. Instead of merely orchestrating tasks and files, Omnipy is an interactive safety net protecting the data itself. Acting as strict border control, it ensures metadata fed into machine learning models is perfectly validated against explicit schemas, and actively catches AI hallucinations before they cause harm. Researchers can experiment freely, knowing their data integrity is constantly guarded.

To explain this architecture, our poster presents an epic comic-book showdown: the "UnFAIR Alliance" (villains of data decay) versus "The Omnificents" (dataflow defenders). Attendees will see these battles play out as our heroes deploy Omnipy's core features:

* **Optimus Parse vs. Lord Franken-Format:**

  Chaotic, unstructured database payloads are elegantly parsed into AI-ready tabular formats via robust declarative conversions.

* **The Endless Schema vs. "Honest" HAL Lucinator:**

  Strict type enforcement acts as an undeniable cosmic law, automatically banishing fabricated keys and AI hallucinations from metadata payloads.

* **The Merciless Mimic Mantis vs. The Null Ninja:**

  Continuous validation natively embedded in camouflaged Python structures protects Jupyter sandboxes, instantly reverting states the microsecond silent corruption occurs.
 
* **Visor vs. Count Cryptic:**
  
  Visor shatters unreadable memory pointers by projecting data in its true multi-dimensional form, using auto-scaling nested panels and highly personalized syntax themes.

While The Omnificents are formidable, the war against the UnFAIR Alliance is ongoing. Omnipy is an open, actively evolving framework, and we cannot protect the bioinformatics ecosystem alone. We invite developers, domain experts, and researchers across ELIXIR to join our heroic roster. Collaborate with us to expand domain models, build plugins, and forge the hallucination-proof foundation needed to safely scale the next generation of AI-assisted science.
